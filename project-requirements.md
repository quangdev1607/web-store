**Snack Food E-commerce Website for Children**

**Technologies Used:**
- .NET Core (for api)
- SQLite

**Features:**

1. **Product and Category Management**
   - 1.1. **View/List Products/Categories:** Display lists with pagination, search, and filtering (by category, price, etc.).
   - 1.2. **Add/Edit Products/Categories:** Manage detailed information (Name, Description, Price, Stock Quantity, Category, Images, Status, etc.).
   - 1.3. **Delete Products/Categories:** Remove or disable products (disable/hide).

2. **Customer Account Management (Registration/Login)**
   - 2.1. **Account Registration:** Store basic information (Name, Email, Password, Phone Number, Shipping Address, etc.). Email verification (optional).
   - 2.2. **Login/Logout.**
   - 2.3. **Account Management (Customer):** Update personal information and change passwords.
   - 2.4. **Account Management (Admin):** Manage user accounts on the website.

3. **Product Viewing and Shopping Cart**
   - 3.1. **Product Page:** Display detailed product information, multiple images, and related details (Category, Description).
   - 3.2. **Add to Cart:** Allow logged-in customers to add products to their cart.
   - 3.3. **Cart Management:** View, update quantities, and remove products from the cart.

4. **Checkout Process**
   - 4.1. **Select Address/Payment Method:** Enter/select shipping address and choose payment method (Cash on Delivery - COD as default).
   - 4.2. **Create Order:** Save order details (Customer, Products, Total Amount, Address, Status, etc.).

5. **Order Management (Admin/Customer)**
   - 5.1. **View Orders (Customer):** Customers can view their order history and order details.
   - 5.2. **Order Management (Admin):** List, search, and filter orders. View order details.
   - 5.3. **Update Order Status (Admin):** Change order status (e.g., Pending, Shipping, Completed, Canceled, etc.).
